# StudentSphere - Learning Management & Campus Assistance Platform

## Overview

StudentSphere is a web-based Learning Management and Campus Assistance Platform developed using the MERN stack. The platform provides a centralized environment where students and faculty can interact, access educational resources, manage academic content, and receive instant support through an AI-powered chatbot.

## Problem Statement

Educational resources are often scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult for students to access study materials, video lectures, and academic support efficiently. StudentSphere addresses this challenge by providing a single platform for learning and campus-related assistance.

## Objectives

* Provide a centralized learning platform for students and faculty.
* Enable seamless access to study materials and video content.
* Integrate AI-powered assistance for academic and campus-related queries.
* Improve student engagement and learning experiences.
* Simplify content sharing and resource management.

## Features

### Faculty Module

* Upload video lectures.
* Share study materials and notes.
* Manage educational content.

### Student Module

* Access video lectures and learning resources.
* Download notes and study materials.
* View YouTube-integrated educational content.
* Interact with the AI chatbot for assistance.

### AI Chatbot

* 24/7 academic support.
* Campus-related information and guidance.
* Quick responses to frequently asked questions.

### Resource Management

* Google Drive integration for downloadable notes.
* Centralized repository for learning materials.
